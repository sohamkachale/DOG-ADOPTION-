Adopt a Friend

Find your perfect furry companion and give them a second chance at happiness

At RescueBuddy, we make adoption easy and heartwarming. Our goal is to help you find the perfect furry companion who fits your lifestyle and brings joy to your home.

How It Works

1

Browse & Explore

View our adoptable dogs and read their unique stories to find your perfect match.

2

Submit Application

Complete our online adoption application to begin the process.

3

Meet & Greet

Schedule a meeting with your potential new family member.

4

Home Check

We ensure your home is safe and suitable for your new pet.

5

Finalize Adoption

Complete the paperwork and welcome your new best friend home!

6

Follow-up Support

We provide ongoing support and resources for you and your pet.

Why Adopt?

By adopting, you're saving a life, reducing shelter overcrowding, and making a lifelong bond with a dog in need.

Save a Life

You're directly saving an animal from euthanasia or extended shelter life.

Make Space

Your adoption creates space for another animal in need at the shelter.

Unmatched Love

Rescue animals are incredibly grateful and form deep bonds with their adopters.

Adoption Requirements

  • You must be at least 21 years old
  • Provide valid identification with current address
  • All household members must agree to the adoption
  • If you rent, provide landlord's contact information and pet policy
  • Be prepared for a home visit by one of our volunteers
  • Pay the adoption fee which covers vaccinations, spay/neuter, and microchipping
