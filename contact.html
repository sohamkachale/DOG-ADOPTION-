Frequently Asked Questions

The adoption process typically takes 1-2 weeks from application to bringing your new friend home. This includes the application review, meet-and-greet, home check, and finalizing paperwork.

Volunteers must be at least 18 years old, complete an orientation session, and commit to a minimum of 4 hours per month. Background checks are required for certain roles involving animal handling or transport.

While we don't provide emergency veterinary services directly, we maintain a network of trusted veterinary partners and can guide you to appropriate emergency care resources. For urgent medical issues, please contact your local emergency veterinary clinic.
