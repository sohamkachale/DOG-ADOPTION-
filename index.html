About RescueBuddy

We believe every dog deserves a loving home and every human deserves a loyal companion.

Founded with a passion for animal welfare, RescueBuddy is more than just a dog adoption platform — it's a community of pet lovers, rescuers, and volunteers working together to give homeless and abandoned dogs a second chance at life.
