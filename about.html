About RescueBuddy

Learn more about our mission, story, and the team behind our work

At RescueBuddy, we believe every dog deserves a loving home and every human deserves a loyal companion.

Our Story

Founded with a passion for animal welfare, RescueBuddy is more than just a dog adoption platform — it's a community of pet lovers, rescuers, and volunteers working together to give homeless and abandoned dogs a second chance at life.

Our journey began in 2015 when our founder, Sarah Johnson, rescued a stray Labrador mix from a busy highway. That experience opened her eyes to the countless dogs in need of help and loving homes. What started as a small initiative to help local shelters has grown into a comprehensive platform connecting thousands of dogs with their forever families.

Our Mission

To connect rescue dogs with compassionate humans, creating lifelong friendships.

What We Do

Partner with Trusted Shelters

We collaborate with reputable rescue organizations and shelters to increase the visibility of adoptable dogs.

Showcase Adoptable Dogs

We create detailed, heartfelt profiles to help potential adopters find their perfect match based on lifestyle and needs.

Promote Responsible Ownership

We provide education and support to ensure successful adoptions and lifelong pet relationships.

Support Foster Programs

We facilitate temporary homes for dogs awaiting their forever families through our foster network.

Our Values

Compassion

We approach every animal and person with empathy, understanding, and kindness.

Responsibility

We take our duty to animals seriously, ensuring their wellbeing is always our priority.

Collaboration

We work together with shelters, volunteers, and communities to maximize our impact.
